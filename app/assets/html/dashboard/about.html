Journalists, broadcasters, & producers

You record and tell stories — you don’t archive them. But memories fade, and material can easily get lost or degrade. When it’s easy to archive, it’s easy to find the threads that create stories.

Save everything.

Create a free account and start uploading. Archive entire collections of raw audio, and completed work. No software to install, no server to manage.

Sound indexed automatically.

All kinds of sound collections benefit from immediate searchability, marked by time-stamps so you can find — and hear — exactly what you’re looking for. We detect keywords in your audio and generate standards-compliant metadata (PBCore).

Liberate treasure troves of sound.

Robust search is great for research and production. Explore your sounds and the public repository by keyword, date, contributor, location, and more. Trapped voices from around the world are waiting to be discovered.

Open to all, or keep it to yourself

Producers and newsrooms can privately access their own content anywhere, anytime. Archives can add material to a searchable, streaming repository.
